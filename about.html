Our Vision
At Sabon Depot Antipolo, we aim to deliver artisinal quality, but affordable cleaning solutions for local and international market. Our dedication to quality craftmanship and sustainability for the environment excels through our driver workforce adhering to strong values of good corporate governance that sets us apart as a trusted purveyor.


Our Mission
To provide cheap and affordable yet high-quality products that promotes wellness by offering innovative and curated selection of products. We strive to inspire eco-friendliness with the use of our refilling and re-using of plastic containers system. We aim to harness a motivated and equipped workforce which gives recognition to employees' skills and competency.




"A healthier home begins with a cleaner conscience. Clear your space, clear your mind, and let the freshness of a clean environment inspire your everyday well-being."
